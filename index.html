What I know

Front End Development

I love to code things from the ground up. This is something that brings me joy when I create something new and useful. Languages that I use to achieve this are HTML, CSS, SASS, JavaScript. The dev tools that I utilize in my work are VSCode, GitHub, Bootstrap, TailwindCSS, and Figma.

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

As a fundamental part of any website nowadays, I find it valuable to learn how to optimize my website so that it is visible to anyone on the world wide web. This is critically important knowledge to have in your arsenal as a web developer.

Backend Development

As the back-end is not as visual as the front end, I shied from it at first. However, I am getting the hang of it and actually enjoy working with it. At first, Node.js was a little daunting for a visual learner such as myself. But now, I find it to be fun to work with. I'm looking forward to broadening my knowledge even more when it comes to back-end technologies.
